CAIO Features

CAIO (Cognitive AI Officer) is your always-on strategic partner across Finance, HR, Ops, Marketing, and Product.

What you can do

  • Unified Dashboard — Upload a document (PDF, PPT, XLSX, DOCX) or paste text and get instant analysis.
  • CXO Brains — CFO, CHRO, COO, CMO, CPO, EA; each provides domain-specific insights.
  • Collective Insights — Get 2–3 cross-functional insights plus 1 recommendation per brain.
  • Chat (Pro+ & Premium) — Ask follow-ups, run scenarios, refine actions.
  • Export — Copy summaries or export to PDF (and Excel where enabled).
  • Privacy-first — Role-based access and clear audit trails. On-prem options for Enterprise.

Supported inputs

  • Documents: PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX
  • Text: pasted notes, briefings, emails
